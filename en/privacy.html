02What we collect

We collect only what we need to understand who visits the site and to continue the conversation when you message us. Here is the full list.

Information you give us directly

  • Your name and phone number, when you start a WhatsApp conversation with us
  • The content of messages you send to us through WhatsApp, SMS, or email
  • Anything else you choose to share during our conversations (cooking preferences, location for shipping or demos, availability, etc.)

Information collected automatically

04Cookies and tracking

We use a small number of cookies and tracking pixels to understand how the site is used and to measure advertising effectiveness.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to count visits, see which pages get attention, and understand traffic sources. Google Analytics may set cookies on your browser. The data is aggregated and does not identify you personally to us.

Advertising

We use the Meta Pixel (Facebook/Instagram) to measure which ads bring visitors to the site and to show you relevant ads on Meta platforms after you visit. The pixel sets cookies controlled by Meta.

Functional

The site loads fonts from Google Fonts. Google may log basic request information when fonts are served. We do not control this directly.

How to opt out

You can refuse or block cookies in your browser settings. You can also opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on. You can adjust your Meta ad preferences in your Facebook or Instagram account settings.

06WhatsApp and Bella

The primary way we talk to you is WhatsApp. When you click any "Chat with me" button on this site, you are taken to WhatsApp with a pre-filled message. Sending that message starts a conversation with us.

Heads up about Bella

The first part of your WhatsApp conversation is handled by Bella, an AI assistant that runs on top of Anthropic's Claude model. Bella reads your messages, generates responses, and may store the conversation history so we can pick up where you left off. A real person (Natascha) reviews conversations and joins in personally when relevant.

Bella exists to give you fast, accurate answers. She does not make purchases on your behalf, does not request sensitive information (passwords, payment details, government IDs), and will hand you over to a human whenever you ask.

The full content of WhatsApp messages flows through Meta's WhatsApp Business infrastructure, which is end-to-end encrypted between you and our number. We retain a copy of the conversation for context and follow-up.

07How long we keep data

  • WhatsApp conversations and contact details: kept while you are an active lead or customer, plus up to 24 months after your last interaction, unless you ask us to delete sooner
  • Analytics data (Google Analytics): aggregated for up to 14 months, then deleted automatically
  • Meta Pixel data: retained by Meta per their policy, typically up to 180 days for ad attribution
  • Email correspondence: kept while relevant, deleted on request

If you ask us to delete your data, we will do so within 30 days, except where we are legally required to keep certain records.

08Your rights

Depending on where you live, you have certain rights regarding your personal information.

If you live in the United States

Under laws like the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and similar state laws (Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, and others), you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we have collected about you
  • Request a copy of that information
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Ask us to delete your information
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information (we do not sell, but you can still opt out of analytics and advertising cookies)
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising any of these rights

If you live in the European Union or UK

Under the GDPR and UK GDPR, you have the rights above plus the right to data portability, the right to object to processing, and the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

How to use these rights

Email support@getsmartcooking.com with your request. We will verify your identity (usually by confirming a detail from a previous conversation) and respond within 30 days.

09Children's privacy

This site is intended for adults 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has shared information with us, contact support@getsmartcooking.com and we will delete it.
